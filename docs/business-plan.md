# Agency Business Plan: AI Answering + Instant Quote SaaS
## For Garage Door Repair Contractors (Expandable)

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Build and sell two complementary products to home-service contractors, starting with garage door repair companies:
1. **Instant Quote Generator** — embeds on the contractor's website, collects visitor details, returns a ballpark quote, and captures the lead.
2. **AI Voice Answering Service** — inbound-only AI receptionist that answers calls 24/7, logs leads, and patches urgent callers through to the owner.

You own the tools, invoice the contractors, and operate as a modern micro-SaaS agency.

---

## 2. Problem
- 78% of customers hire the first company that quotes them.
- 24-hour callback = lost lead.
- After-hours voicemail = lost revenue.
- Most small contractors cannot afford a full-time dispatcher or answering service.

---

## 3. Target Market
- **Primary**: Garage door repair companies (1–5 trucks, owner-operated).
- **Why this niche**: High-ticket jobs ($400–$2,500), high after-hours urgency, and less private-equity saturation than HVAC, plumbing, or electrical.
- **Beachhead**: A single metro area (e.g., DFW) to build local credibility and case studies.

---

## 4. Product Details

### A. Instant Quote Generator
- Multi-step form on contractor's website → instant estimate → lead capture.
- Admin dashboard for the contractor to see leads, quotes, and basic analytics.
- Mobile-friendly, embeddable via iframe or hosted subdomain.
- Pricing: $79–$199/month or outright sale ($500–$2,000).

### B. AI Voice Answering Service
- Inbound calls only initially.
- Simple script: greet → collect name + address/issue → promise callback.
- Urgent-detection logic: if caller sounds highly motivated (e.g., "flooding," "can't leave") → patch to owner.
- Pricing: $49–$149/month + $20–$50 per closed lead, or retainer-only.

### C. Job Profit Tracker (Future Add-On)
- Cost/margin calculator per job.
- Lower urgency to build; higher-ticket expansion later.

---

## 5. Go-To-Market

### Lead Sourcing
Scrape Google Maps for garage door businesses; filter for mobile numbers only.

### Outreach Flow
1. **Warm validation text**: "Hey [City], I found you on Google Maps. Do you still fix garage doors?"
2. **Interest responses**: Short pitch for AI answering service with risk reversal: no upfront cost, no contract, free setup, $X per lead or flat monthly fee.
3. **Close for pilot**: 14-day free trial; convert to paid if they receive at least one closed lead.

### Sales Model
- Early stage: cost-per-lead to validate demand.
- Mature stage: MRR retainer + overage or tiered plans.

---

## 6. Operations Plan
- **Team**: 1–2 founders/operators initially.
- **Required hours**: 2–4 hours/day during validation; scalable to part-time staff later.
- **Tasks**: respond to texts, close pilots, maintain apps, handle support, iterate quoting logic.

## 8. Risks & Mitigations
| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **TCPA/SMS compliance** | Use compliant platforms, short codes or verified sender IDs, clear opt-out language. |
| **Spam filtering / deliverability** | Personalize texts, rotate sender pools, avoid bulk blasts without personalization. |
| **AI misrouting or bad UX** | Strict script, high-quality voice model, easy human-forwarding logic. |
| **Competition from PE-backed players** | Focus on micro-contractors ignored by large platforms. |
| **No-code vendor lock-in** | Export code/data regularly; use standards-based platforms when possible. |
| **Churn if results are poor** | Guarantee first lead or money back; measure lead quality closely. |

---

## 9. Key Performance Indicators
- Texts sent → responses → interest → pilot signups → paid clients
- Response rate target: 30–60%
- Interest rate target: 10–20%
- Pilot-to-paid rate target: 30–50%
- Cost per acquired client (CAC): target < $150
- Lifetime value (LTV): target > $1,000
