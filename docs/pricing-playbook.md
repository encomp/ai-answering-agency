# Pricing Playbook

## Purpose
This document defines exactly what we sell, at what price, and under what terms. It exists so we can close pilots, avoid disputes, and raise prices later without renegotiating individually.

---

## 1. Offer Tiers

### Tier 1: Pay-Per-Lead
- **Price**: $50 per closed lead
- **Billing**: Weekly invoice; Net 7
- **Includes**: AI voice answering, call recording, lead summary email, basic CRM webhook
- **Excludes**: quote tool, profit tracker, custom workflow automation
- **Best for**: skeptical owners who want zero risk
- **Minimum commitment**: none

### Tier 2: Monthly Retainer
- **Price**: $149/month
- **Billing**: Monthly auto-pay; Net 30
- **Includes**: AI voice answering 24/7, dedicated local number, call recording, lead summary email, basic CRM webhook, up to 200 minutes/month
- **Overage**: $0.25/minute beyond 200 minutes
- **Excludes**: quote tool, profit tracker
- **Best for**: owners who want simple predictable billing
- **Minimum commitment**: 1 month

### Tier 3: Full Stack (Recommended)
- **Price**: $299/month
- **Billing**: Monthly auto-pay; Net 30
- **Includes**: everything in Tier 2 + instant quote generator embedded on their website + profit tracker dashboard
- **Overage**: $0.25/minute beyond 300 minutes
- **Best for**: owners who want the complete system
- **Minimum commitment**: 1 month

### One-Time Setup Fee
- $0 for pilots.
- $500 after pilot if they join paid.
- Waived for annual prepay.

---

## 2. Closed Lead Definition

A "closed lead" is a caller who:
1. reaches the AI during or after hours,
2. provides a name and serviceable address,
3. has a legitimate garage door issue,
4. and either:
   - books a job, or
   - requests a callback and the owner/crew makes contact within 24 hours.

Disqualifiers:
- wrong number / hang-up within 10 seconds
- solicitors, spam, obvious scams
- duplicate entry for an already-recorded job
- test calls by us or the owner

**Dispute rule**: contractor must notify us within 48 hours of the call summary email if a lead should be disqualified. If no dispute is raised, the lead is considered closed and billable.

---

## 3. Pilot Program

**Terms**:
- 14 days free.
- Limit: 5 pilots active at once during validation (can expand later).
- Pilot includes Tier 2 features only.
- No payment required during pilot.
- At pilot end, convert to paid or cancel with zero obligation.

**Conversion goal**:
- Pilot must receive at least 1 closed lead to qualify for conversion offer.
- If zero closed leads, offer extension or graceful exit.

**Owner requirements during pilot**:
- Keep the dedicated number active.
- Notify us within 48 hours of any forwarding/missed-call issues.
- Confirm or dispute leads within 48 hours via the summary email.

---

## 4. Payment Flow

### Pay-Per-Lead
1. Week ends Sunday 11:59 PM.
2. We send invoice Monday morning with that week's closed leads.
3. Payment due within 7 days.
4. Methods: ACH, card via Stripe/Paddle invoice, or PayPal.
5. Late fee: 1.5% per month on balances >14 days overdue.

### Retainer / Full Stack
1. Auto-pay on the 1st of each month.
2. prorated if starting mid-month.
3. First invoice includes first month + any setup fee, unless waived.
4. Annual prepay: 10% discount.

---

## 5. Number Porting / Setup

- We provide a new local number or can port an existing number.
- Porting usually takes 5–10 business days.
- During porting, we temporarily forward the old number to the new AI line.
- We own the number while the client is active; if they cancel, they may port it away within 30 days at no charge.
- If they do not port it within 30 days of cancellation, the number returns to our pool.

---

## 6. Cancellation

### Monthly Plans
- Cancel anytime with 30 days' written notice.
- Access continues until end of paid period.
- No refunds for partial months.

### Annual Plans
- Cancellation effective at term end.
- No refunds for unused months.

### Data Export
- Upon cancellation, owner may request:
  - CSV of all leads (name, phone, address, call summary)
  - Recordings for 90 days after request
- We retain anonymized data for QA and model improvement unless owner opts out.

---

## 7. Support & Uptime

- **Support**: email/chat during business hours; 24-hour response target.
- **Uptime target**: 99% during business hours; 95% overall including after-hours AI.
- **SLA credit**: if AI is down >4 hours in a month, owner gets 10% off next month.
- **Exclusions**: scheduled maintenance, carrier outages, owner-provided number/network issues.

---

## 8. Plan Change / Upgrade / Downgrade

- Upgrades: effective immediately; prorated billing.
- Downgrades: effective at next billing cycle.
- Switching from pay-per-lead to retainer: one-time $250 "conversion credit" applied to first month.

---

## 9. Prohibited Uses

Owner may not:
- use the number or service for illegal activity
- resell or white-label without a separate agreement
- use recorded voices for non-garage-door purposes without written consent
- attempt to reverse-engineer or extract the AI model/prompts

Violation = immediate termination, no refund.

## 11. Pricing Evolution Rules

- Do not discount below $50/lead or $149/mo without founder approval.
- First 5 pilot clients: full Tier 2 free, no obligation.
- After 10 paying clients: reevaluate pricing in a dedicated meeting.
- Never lower price for a single client unless it unlocks a case study or referral.

---

## 12. Key Metrics to Track Per Client

- Minutes used vs plan limit
- Closed leads per week / month
- Revenue per client (RPC)
- Support tickets / churn signals
- Referral requests sent

This data will justify future price increases.
