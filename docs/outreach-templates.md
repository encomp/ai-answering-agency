# Outreach Templates

## Core Principles
- Short. < 150 words.
- One clear ask.
- No automation-sounding language.
- Unsubscribe / opt-out on every outbound message.

---

## 1. Cold Email — First Touch

**Subject**: Quick question about [Company Name]'s after-hours calls

> Hi [First Name],
>
> I'm in Dallas and I came across [Company Name] on Google Maps. Looks like you've been in business for a while.
>
> I'm testing something for local garage door repair shops: an AI receptionist that answers calls 24/7, collects the job details, and either forwards urgent calls to you live or logs them for first-thing callback.
>
> Would it be useful to see a 60-second demo?
>
> If not, no worries at all.
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]

## 6. Demo Recording Script (for Loom)

1. **0:00–0:05** — Hook: "I built this for a local garage door owner. It answers after-hours calls so he doesn't miss jobs."
2. **0:05–0:15** — Show phone calling the business line. AI answers on the first ring.
3. **0:15–0:35** — Fake caller: "My door won't open and I need to leave in 10 minutes. I'm at 123 Main St."
4. **0:35–0:45** — AI replies: "Got it. I'm sending this to [Owner] right now. He'll call you in 2 minutes."
5. **0:45–0:55** — Show owner's phone lighting up with the forwarded call or show the lead logged in the CRM.
6. **0:55–1:00** — Close: "That's it. 24/7, no missed calls, no extra hire."

---

## 7. Key Phrases to Use

**Good**:
- "AI receptionist"
- "after-hours call coverage"
- "log leads for first-thing callback"
- "forward urgent calls live"
- "free 14-day pilot"
- "no contract, no setup fee"

**Avoid**:
- "automated spam"
- "robocall"
- "we'll sell you leads"
- "telemarketing"
- "guaranteed jobs"

---

## 8. Legal / Compliance Checklist

Before sending any outreach:
- [ ] Review `docs/compliance.md`
- [ ] Include physical mailing address in every email footer
- [ ] Include clear unsubscribe mechanism (reply "STOP" or click link)
- [ ] Verify email list sources and terms of use
- [ ] If texting: confirm express written consent exists per TCPA 1:1 requirement

---

## Notes
- Personalize every email with one real detail from their Google Maps / website profile.
- Reply to every response, even "not interested," within 24 hours.
- Track opens, replies, and positive responses so we can A/B test subject lines and offers.
